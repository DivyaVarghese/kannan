About Us

Twiclo Fudzz is a food delivering website, where people use it to order food online and makes it get delivered at home. It has got a lot of functions in it. This is a responsive website that supports both desktop and mobile platforms, where people can order and get food that is delicious and lip-smacking. This helps the customers to choose from a variety of cuisines like Italian, Continental, American, Chinese, Japanese, North Indian and South Indian.It is one of the web applications that changed the tradition of visiting the restaurants in person.

The application is built in a robust way in order to handle a lot of customers in one go . This helps the management to serve the customers in a fast and reliable way to deliver the orders. This app has two modules that are vendor module and the customer module. The customer modules handle the customers, where the vendor module handles and process the orders. The customer module is where the customer orders the food by the listings that are arrayed in the application.

We give prominence to the quality of food during the process of delivering. And we are more concerned about building the healthy customer and vendor relationship. Customer satisfaction is the prime importance for our brand; this makes us handle the queries and complaints of the customers as soon as possible. Along with the services we never fail to provide feedback options for the customers in order to enhance ourselves and to serve better. We are serving round the clock to the customers in order to satisfy the appetite, and along with the delivering the food, we never failed to deliver the affection and love.
