Cooking Perfect Fried Rice in minutes

1) In a saucepan, bring 1 cup of chicken broth to a boil. Add 1 1/3 cup Minute Rice, remove from heat and cover for 5 minutes.
2) Chop a small onion while heating 1 T butter over medium-high heat in a large nonstick pan. Add the egg and break it up to scramble it and add the onion right away also. Cook until the egg is done and onion clearing. About 3 minutes.
3) Add the rice to the egg/onion along with 3 T soya sauce.
4) “Fry” for 3-4 minutes over medium-high heat.
5) Serve. Done in 10 minutes.

Secret of making Heart Shaped eggs

1) Place your egg in boiling water and leave to boil for 5-10 minutes
2) Once the egg is cooked, run it under cold water and leave it to cool
3) Meanwhile, cut then ends off your empty juice carton, then cut off one side so you left with half the carton and you can fold it in half (see the video below if you're not sure what to do)
4) Now peel the boiled egg and sit it in the middle of the cardboard, so the card is folded up over each side of the egg
5) Place your chopstick/pen on top of the egg so that it creates a groove in the top - hold the chopstick/pen firmly in place with two elastic bands wrapped around the card Leave it to rest for 10 minutes
6) Now remove the bands and chopstick/pen and you should have a lovely heart-shaped egg Carefully cut it in half and serve with toast or salad
